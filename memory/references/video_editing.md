## Short Form (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)

- Hook in first 3 seconds: bold statement, surprising fact, or emotional reaction
- 85% of viewers watch without sound — captions are non-negotiable
- Bold styled captions (Hormozi-style yellow highlights or MrBeast bold white text)
- Optimal clip length: 30-60 seconds outperforms 15 seconds for engagement
- 9:16 vertical format for Reels/TikTok
- Find emotional moments: laughter, heated debate, inspiration — not random timestamps
- Cuts every 2-4 seconds to maintain attention

## Long Form (YouTube, full podcast episodes)

- First 30 seconds must hook — bold statement, question, or surprise
- Keep 80% of viewers past the 15-second mark or algorithm punishes reach
- B-roll, reaction shots, zoom-ins break up talking heads
- J-cuts and L-cuts keep audio flowing naturally between scenes
- Chapters/timestamps help retention
- Audio quality = 50% of the experience
- Match cuts create "flow state" — 10min feels like 2min

## Transitions by Platform

**TikTok** (raw, fast, authentic):
- Jump cuts (most used)
- Whip pans
- Hand/object swipes across lens
- Glitch effects
- Over-polished = feels fake

**Instagram Reels** (slightly polished):
- Smooth zoom transitions
- Spin transitions (slow spin dominating 2026)
- Match cuts synced to music beats
- Hand swipe reveals

**YouTube** (substance over style):
- J-cuts and L-cuts
- Subtle zoom punches on key points
- B-roll cutaways instead of hard cuts
- Too many flashy transitions = looks amateur

## Podcast Clips Specifically

- Best clips: emotional moments, laughter, heated debate, inspiration
- Lead with the payoff — not the setup
- Captions serve both accessibility and Instagram SEO/algorithm ranking
- 30-90 seconds for LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
- Remove silences with auto-editor for tighter pacing
