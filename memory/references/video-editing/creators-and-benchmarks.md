## Podcast Creators

### Radiolab (Jad Abumrad)
- **Niche**: Science, philosophy, human experience
- **Editing signature**: Layered sound design — "jaggedy sounds, little plurps, strange staccato, percussive things" woven as thematic soundscapes. The edit IS the storytelling.
- **What to study**: How sound design carries narrative meaning, not just decoration. Music and effects as argument.
- **Awards**: 2 Peabody Awards, 7 podcast industry awards

### This American Life (Ira Glass)
- **Niche**: Narrative nonfiction, feature documentary audio
- **Editing signature**: Clean narrative structure with act breaks. Music helps "make your point," never just decoration. Discards ~80% of recorded interviews.
- **What to study**: Narrative structure, ruthless cutting, music as editorial tool
- **Equipment**: Marantz PMD661 recorders, Audio Technica AT835b shotgun mics

### 99% Invisible (Roman Mars)
- **Niche**: Design, architecture, everyday objects
- **Editing signature**: Rhythm and musicality in spoken-word storytelling. Sound-rich descriptions of visual subjects.
- **What to study**: Making audio vivid without visuals. Pacing as music.
- **Stats**: 625+ episodes, nearly 97% five-star ratings

### Serial (Sarah Koenig)
- **Niche**: Investigative journalism
- **Editing signature**: Serialized narrative with cliffhangers. Original scores. Pioneered the genre.
- **What to study**: Long-arc tension, pacing across episodes, keeping listeners hooked week-to-week

### The Daily (NYT)
- **Niche**: News, current events
- **Editing signature**: Recognizable theme music (by Jim Brunberg & Ben Landsverk). Clean, efficient daily format. 2M downloads per weekday.
- **What to study**: Theme music as brand identity. Efficient daily production workflow.

### Darknet Diaries (Jack Rhysider)
- **Niche**: Cybersecurity, hacking, true crime
- **Editing signature**: Solo production (first 40 episodes). Hypnotic narration with deep expertise. Theme by Breakmaster Cylinder.
- **What to study**: What one person can produce at professional quality. Narration pacing.

### Joe Rogan Experience
- **Niche**: Long-form conversation
- **Editing signature**: Minimal editing — raw, uncut conversations. Multi-camera studio in Austin. Template for modern video podcasts.
- **What to study**: When NOT to edit. Authenticity as a production value.
- **Producer**: Jamie Vernon (since 2012)

### Lex Fridman Podcast
- **Niche**: Science, technology, philosophy
- **Editing signature**: Minimalist aesthetic. Dark, moody studio lighting. Clean multi-camera. 2-4+ hour interviews.
- **What to study**: Letting conversations breathe. Visual consistency as brand. Less is more.

### Colin and Samir
- **Niche**: Creator economy, media business
- **Editing signature**: Polished video production with dynamic editing — frequent B-roll, graphics overlays, data visualizations, tight pacing.
- **What to study**: How to make a conversation visually dynamic without feeling forced. Strong clip pipeline.
- **Stats**: 1M+ YouTube subscribers

### Huberman Lab
- **Niche**: Health, neuroscience
- **Editing signature**: Clean presentation with graphics, diagrams, and supplementary visuals during explanations. Chapter markers.
- **What to study**: Educational podcast formatting, visual aids for complex topics

## Short-Form Creators

### Sam Kolder (@samkolder)
- **Niche**: Cinematic travel
- **Editing signature**: Pioneer of teal-orange travel edits, hyperlapse transitions, match cuts, speed ramps. Music-driven editing.
- **What to study**: Speed ramps, match cuts between locations, color grading for mood. Every transition has purpose.

### Daniel Schiffer (@danielschiffer)
- **Niche**: Product videography
- **Editing signature**: Cinematic lighting, macro transitions, product interactions with foley sound design. Makes everyday products look premium.
- **What to study**: B-roll composition, sound design for product interaction, how lighting creates mood

### Alex Hormozi (@hormozi)
- **Niche**: Business, entrepreneurship
- **Editing signature**: Bold kinetic text, rapid jump cuts, emoji overlays, zoom punches on key words. Defined the modern podcast-clip editing language.
- **What to study**: Kinetic typography pacing, how text reinforces speech, high-energy talking-head editing
- **Note**: Team of 2-5 editors producing 3-7 Reels daily

### Chris Williamson (@chriswillx)
- **Niche**: Psychology, self-improvement
- **Editing signature**: Aggressive zoom-ins, dynamic text animations, bass-boosted sound effects. High-energy podcast clips.
- **What to study**: Sound effect placement for impact, zoom timing, caption animation styles

### GaryVee (@garyvee)
- **Niche**: Business, motivation
- **Editing signature**: High-volume raw energy with bold captions. Quantity-driven with consistent brand identity.
- **What to study**: Batch production workflow, maintaining quality at high volume

## YouTube Creators

### MrBeast (Jimmy Donaldson)
- **Niche**: Entertainment, challenges, philanthropy
- **Editing signature**: Retention editing — "loud sound effects, fast cuts, flashing lights and zero pauses." Premise within 30 seconds. $1M+ per flagship video. 250+ employees.
- **What to study**: Hook construction, payoff loops, pattern interrupts. Also study his evolution — he's moving away from "ultra fast-paced/overstim" toward more deliberate pacing.
- **Notable editors**: Matt Turner (2018-2019), Nate Anderson

### MKBHD (Marques Brownlee)
- **Niche**: Tech reviews
- **Editing signature**: Clean, cinematic. RED cameras. Final Cut Pro X. Controlled color grading, precise product shots, smooth camera movements, minimal clutter.
- **What to study**: How restraint creates quality. Product B-roll composition. Color consistency across videos.
- **Studio**: Kearny, NJ

### Casey Neistat
- **Niche**: Vlog, filmmaking, storytelling
- **Editing signature**: Raw authenticity with fast energy. Inspirational interludes. First-person autobiographical storytelling. Used FCP 7, then Premiere Pro.
- **What to study**: Vlog pacing as art form, how to make daily life cinematic, music selection

### Kurzgesagt — In a Nutshell
- **Niche**: Science education, animation
- **Editing signature**: Flat vector illustrations with cosmic color palettes. Minimalistic animated educational content. Team of ~70. Uses After Effects and Illustrator.
- **What to study**: Branding through consistent visual style. Complex topics made accessible through animation.

### Veritasium (Derek Muller)
- **Niche**: Science education
- **Editing signature**: Mixes public interviews, expert conversations, experiments, and dramatizations. Emmy-nominated.
- **What to study**: How to blend formats (interview + demonstration + graphics) in one video

### Every Frame a Painting (Tony Zhou & Taylor Ramos)
- **Niche**: Film analysis, video essays
- **Editing signature**: Precise visual analysis timed to narration. Gold standard for film criticism video essays. Influenced filmmaker Christopher McQuarrie.
- **What to study**: Why editing works. This channel teaches the theory behind the craft. Essential viewing for any editor.
- **Note**: Active 2014-2016, revived 2024

### Corridor Digital / Corridor Crew
- **Niche**: VFX, filmmaking
- **Editing signature**: Pop-culture VFX short films. "Bosstown Dynamics" showed convincing robot VFX. Accessible VFX breakdowns.
- **What to study**: Practical vs digital VFX, what's possible with consumer tools

### Johnny Harris
- **Niche**: Geopolitics, journalism
- **Editing signature**: Fast-paced visual storytelling. Heavy custom map animations, archival footage. Former Vox Borders (Emmy-nominated).
- **What to study**: Map-based storytelling, pacing for complex topics, using archival footage effectively

### Wendover Productions (Sam Denby)
- **Niche**: Logistics, economics, aviation
- **Editing signature**: Clean map-based motion graphics, data visualization, infographics. 803M+ views.
- **What to study**: Data visualization as storytelling, clean infographic design

### Film Riot (Ryan Connolly)
- **Niche**: Filmmaking education
- **Editing signature**: Practical tutorials covering DIY VFX, cinematography, editing. 1.6M+ subs since 2009.
- **What to study**: Practical filmmaking techniques, bridging education and production

### Peter McKinnon
- **Niche**: Cinematic vlogging, photography, editing education
- **Editing signature**: High production value vlogs that teach while entertaining. Strong transitions and color grading.
- **What to study**: Cinematic B-roll techniques, how to teach editing through content
