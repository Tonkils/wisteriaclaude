## Wisteria Farms Content Framework (Heat & Repeat Strategy)

Learned from Dec 5, 2025 viral content course:

1. **Hook** — First 5 seconds. Curiosity loop. 4 levers: Stakes, Knowledge Gap, Reactions, Great Premise
2. **Eliminate Lulls** — Remove all dead air, filler, anything unnecessary
3. **Add Payoff** — Promise something at the start, deliver at the end
4. **Tap Relatability** — Maryland-specific for BLVCK MRKT; use relatable controversy
5. **Repeat** — Find formats that can be recreated consistently

Key insight: Watch time is KING. Likes/comments/shares matter less than watch time.
Every 4-8 seconds: angle change, controversial take, or new element to reset attention clock.
