Record everything from conversations — the full content, not just highlights or "important" parts.

**Why:** Jaret explicitly requested this. What seems trivial in the moment may matter later. The memory should be a complete, growing record of all work sessions.

**How to apply:** At the end of every session, write a full transcript to sessions/transcripts/. Don't filter or summarize — capture everything that was discussed, decided, coded, or asked about. The session log in sessions/log.md is the summary; the transcript is the full record.
